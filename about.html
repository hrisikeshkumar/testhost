Our Story

8 Years of Connecting Talent with Opportunity

Founded in 2017 in New Delhi, TalentBridge India began with a single mission — to make recruitment honest, efficient, and human. What started as a small team of three passionate HR professionals has grown into one of Delhi NCR's most trusted placement agencies.

Today, we serve 500+ corporate clients across 20+ industries, with a team of 60+ specialized recruiters who bring deep domain expertise to every search we undertake.
